The Anatomy of a Sleeve: Soft Radiance

There is a distinct, rhythmic peace in watching a garment grow, row by row. Right now, my hands are occupied with shaping the sleeves for a new custom sweater, and it's a beautiful study in contrasts.

I wanted to capture the soft transition of morning mist - moving from a deep, grounding espresso brown at the cuffs into a weightless, cloud-like cream. To add a layer of subtle magic, I am weaving in a delicate strand of gold sequins. They catch the light quietly, like morning dew.

Chasing Clouds: The Ombré Knit Is Complete ✨
